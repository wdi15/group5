There are many non-profit organizations out there that help children all over the world have access to education. One of these organizations is Education without borders (EwB) , which is a Canadian non-profit organization. This organization helps with providing schooling for people in places where everything isn't just ponies and rainbows. They do this through their tutoring programs and by focusing on one school at a time. One of these programs include the "math yes we can" tutoring program which enables students to learn highschool math, and has been very successful in fostering interest of learning in children of South Africa.

Some charities have bigger areas of interest like the organization called Concern Worldwide . Concern Worldwide is an organization based in Europe that has been working towards this cause for more than 30 years. Even though their main aim is to provide secure access to education to children worldwide, they also do many things to ensure the safety and well-being of children. They have worked in many countries including but not limited to Ethiopia, Malawi, Bangladesh, Kenya and Sierra Leone. Within the work that Concern Worldwide does, there is a focus on improving the lives of girls and women. Not only is this group working to educate teachers and build schools, they also work with the local governments of some of the poorest countries in the world to challenge inequality, provide basic education to marginalized children, and remove barriers that prevent children from learning.
